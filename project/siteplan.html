Café Aurora — Website Site Plan

simple outline of the pages, navigation, and key sections for the café project.

Site Identity & Purpose

Site Name
Café Aurora — evokes a warm morning start and a welcoming campus spot.
Purpose
Inform students and faculty about the café menu, hours, specials, events, and provide easy ways to contact or place pickup orders.
Target Audience
University students, staff, and visitors looking for coffee, pastries, study-friendly space, and event updates.
Primary Navigation
Home · Menu · About · Contact

Website Structure & Navigation Hierarchy

Pages

  • Home (index.html)
    • Hero / Banner
    • Featured Specials
    • Hours & Location
    • Call-to-Action: View Menu
  • Menu (menu.html)
    • Coffee & Espresso
    • Teas & Cold Drinks
    • Pastries & Snacks
    • Seasonal / Student Picks
  • About (about.html)
    • Our Story
    • Sourcing & Sustainability
    • Gallery (lazy-loaded images)
  • Contact (contact.html)
    • Contact Form
    • Map & Directions
    • Social Links

Shared Layout

  • Header
    • Logo / Site Name
    • Primary Navigation
  • Main
    • Page-specific Sections (see pages)
  • Footer
    • Hours • Address
    • Social • Copyright
    • References Page Link

Visualization uses simple nested lists styled with Flexbox to show hierarchy.

Key Sections per Page

Home

  • Hero with welcoming message
  • Daily/Weekly specials
  • Hours, map snippet
  • CTA buttons: View Menu, Contact

Menu

  • Filter or tabs by category
  • Prices & dietary labels
  • Seasonal highlights

About

  • Story & mission
  • Responsible sourcing
  • Team / café ambience gallery

Contact

  • Accessible contact form
  • Map, address, hours
  • Phone & social links

Typography & Color

Fonts: Lora (headings), Nunito (body). Loaded via Google Fonts.

Palette: Coffee Brown (brand), Cream (background), Forest (accents). Applied to headings, borders, and section accents in this document.

Implementation Notes

  • Minimum three pages with consistent navigation and theme.
  • Standards-based HTML and valid, non-duplicated CSS.
  • Responsive layout; optimized images with lazy loading where appropriate.
  • Include an HTML form (Contact) and basic JavaScript for interactivity & localStorage.
  • Link a References page from the footer.
