Our Story

From a tiny idea to a neighborhood staple—crafted with coffee, care, and community.

How it began

Café Aurora started with a simple goal: brew exceptional coffee and create a welcoming space. What began as weekend pop‑ups grew into a full café shaped by local artists, growers, and regulars.

Today, we focus on seasonal coffees, friendly service, and a calm place to study, meet, or unwind. We’re grateful you’re here.

Sourcing & Sustainability

  • Responsible Sourcing

    We partner with small farms and trusted importers who prioritize fair pricing and quality.

  • Environmental Care

    Compostable cups/lids, careful water use, and a waste‑sorting station reduce our footprint.

  • Local Community

    We showcase local bakers, donate grounds to gardeners, and host student/open‑mic nights.
